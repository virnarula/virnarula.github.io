Microcontroller Developer - Minimize (January 2020 - Present)

Minimize is a wearable device intended for Parkinson's and Essential Tremours patients, which dampens the intensity of the tremours. I have worked on and am currently working on programming the microcontroller on the device to do tasks such as:

  • Track movement through series of sensors and process data in a compressed and efficient mannar
  • Adjusted internal mechanism to adjust for changes in tremour frequency and amplitude
  • Communicate with smartphones in order to allow users to transfer data and track tremours in the smart phone app
